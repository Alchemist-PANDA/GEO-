# Demo simulation: Burger Hub

This is a synthetic product walkthrough, not a customer case study, provider experiment, or claim of measured lift.
“Burger Hub” is a fixture used to exercise the remediation workflow offline.

The included before/after values were assigned by the simulation. They do not demonstrate that structured data,
content, or any BrandSight action changed an AI provider’s output. No provider observations, comparable time periods,
indexing verification, control group, or real deployment evidence support a causal conclusion.

## What this demo is useful for

- Exercising the report and remediation interfaces without credentials.
- Testing that generated artifacts have the expected shape.
- Demonstrating how a future measured experiment could be presented.

## What would be required for a real case study

1. Capture versioned prompts across named live providers before intervention.
2. Store raw observations, provider/model identifiers, timestamps, execution modes, and citations.
3. Verify the intervention was deployed and indexed.
4. Repeat the same prompt/provider sample after a declared waiting period.
5. Report numerator, denominator, provider coverage, failures, uncertainty, and all protocol changes.
6. Describe observed association without claiming causality unless the study design supports it.

Until those steps are completed with a consenting real customer, BrandSight GEO has no validated lift case study.
