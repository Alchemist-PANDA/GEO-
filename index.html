ANSWERS

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?
GEO is the technical discipline of structuring brand signals so that large language models (LLMs) and generative search systems can accurately index, cite, and reference your business context in AI responses.
How does the Lift Simulator predict score improvements?
The simulator models the impact of resolving gaps (like organization schema or localized transparency paragraphs) and calculates the projected coverage index lift using a deterministic semantic alignment algorithm.
Can I export the schema directly to my CMS?
Yes. Deployed remediations in the Remediation Hub can be exported as standard JSON-LD files which can be directly embedded into the head of your HTML documents.
