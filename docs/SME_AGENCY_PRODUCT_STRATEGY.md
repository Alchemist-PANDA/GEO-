# BrandSight GEO — SME and agency product strategy

## Positioning

BrandSight GEO is an evidence-first AI visibility monitor for local businesses,
SMEs, and the marketing agencies that serve them. It should answer:

> What did AI search recommend, what sources influenced it, what is missing for
> this business, and what should we fix this week?

This is deliberately narrower and more actionable than an enterprise command
centre. A local restaurant, clinic, gym, ecommerce store, or agency client needs
clear evidence, prioritized work, and a client-ready report.

## Competitive baseline

Current category leaders commonly offer multi-engine monitoring, prompt libraries,
mention and citation tracking, competitor benchmarking, historical trends, and
agency reporting. Otterly emphasizes accessible monitoring and location-level
insight; Peec emphasizes marketing analytics, competitor benchmarking, and
workflow access; Profound and Scrunch emphasize deeper enterprise coverage and
optimization workflows.

## SME differentiation

1. **Evidence before score** — every percentage links to the exact provider
   response, prompt, timestamp, and source URL.
2. **Local intent** — city, service, language, location, and “near me” prompts
   are first-class inputs.
3. **Action clarity** — recommendations are ranked by impact, effort, and
   evidence; no vague AI-generated task lists.
4. **Agency delivery** — repeatable client snapshots, white-label reports,
   saved prompt sets, and multi-brand workflow without enterprise complexity.
5. **Affordable truth** — fixture/demo mode is useful for evaluation but never
   enters authoritative metrics or customer reports.

## Product guardrails

- Never equate a mention with a recommendation.
- Never claim measured lift from a simulation.
- Never show a zero when there is insufficient evidence.
- Never mix providers, prompts, or periods in a competitor comparison.
- Never silently replace a failed provider response with synthetic data.

## Suggested SME plans

| Plan | Best for | Product promise |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | One SME | Core monitoring, competitor comparison, monthly report |
| Professional | Growing SME or freelancer | More prompts, history, evidence drill-down, action plans |
| Agency | Marketing agency | Multiple client brands, white-label exports, saved templates |

Pricing should be validated through pilots. Usage limits must be enforced by the
same centralized entitlement definitions used by both the API and Streamlit.
